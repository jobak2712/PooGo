# Privacy Policy for PooGo

**Last Updated: December 14, 2024**

## Overview

PooGo ("the App") is a toilet finder application that helps users locate nearby public restrooms. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

### Location Data
- **What**: Your precise GPS location
- **Why**: To find toilets near you
- **Storage**: Location is sent to our servers only during active searches to log which toilet locations are being found. We do not track your location in the background.

### Device Identifier
- **What**: An anonymized device ID (not linked to your identity)
- **Why**: To understand app usage patterns and improve the service
- **Storage**: Stored on our servers with search requests

### Toilet Feedback
- **What**: Your thumbs up/down ratings on toilet locations
- **Why**: To improve toilet recommendations for all users
- **Storage**: Stored on our servers to help other users

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- Your name, email, or contact information
- Your browsing history
- Your photos, contacts, or other personal files
- Background location tracking

## Third-Party Services

- **Apple Maps**: Used to display maps and provide directions. Subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)
- **Supabase**: Our backend database provider. Subject to [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)

## Data Retention

Search request data is retained for analytics purposes. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.

## Children's Privacy

PooGo does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
